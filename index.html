About M&B Auto Body

A premier division of M&B Auto Group since 2005

M&B Auto Group has been serving the community with exceptional auto body repair services for over 15 years. As a family-owned business, we take pride in our craftsmanship and customer service.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with the latest technology and tools to handle any repair job, from minor dents to major collision damage. We work with all makes and models, domestic and imported vehicles.

At M&B Auto Body, we believe in transparency, quality, and building long-term relationships with our customers. That's why we provide detailed estimates, regular updates, and stand behind our work with comprehensive warranties.
